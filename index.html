Krishna Rastogi

Hi, I am Krishna Rastogi. Currently, I am a Visiting Student in Camera Culutre Group at MIT Media Lab where I'm exploring Retinal, Hair and Dental Imaging. At the lab, my work focuses on making low-cost and non-invasive diagnostic medical devices. Along with other team members:

  • Through Retinal Imaging, I am qualitatively analysing the disease state that appears on the retina.
  • Through hair imaging, I'm analysing hair cuticle to differentiate between healthy and damaged hair.
  • Through dential imaging, I am working on predictive analysis of oral health on daily basis.

    • Alongside, I am pursuing my undergrade in biotechnology from PES university, Bangalore.

    During my undergraduate program, I worked on projects involving several disciplines (electronics, environmental, healthcare) to solve real-life problems. My research interest are Low cost Health diagnostic, Environmental Studies, Genetic Engineering, Wearables, Mobile-based technologies, Computational Fluid Dynamics, Optical Engineering , Olfaction studies, Biomedical Imaging. Besides work, I love to travel and admire nature.
