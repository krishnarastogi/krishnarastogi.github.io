Application of Constructed Wetland

A prototype of constructed wetland was designed to treat the municipal waste water. The prototype demonstrated proved to be efficacious in significantly reducing levels of toxicants.Employing this filtration system will enable farmers to use water obtained from a fast, cheap and efficient water recycling unit. We hope to provide a sustainable water solution to the Indian rural community through our system. In this study series of BOD, COD, TSS and TDS is conducted to proof the concept.

Technical Implementations

Analysis of BOD, COD, TSS and TDS, Microbial analysis of soil
