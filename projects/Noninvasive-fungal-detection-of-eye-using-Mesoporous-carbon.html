Noninvasive fungal detection of eye using Mesoporous carbon

The idea is to develop a very low cost non-invasive product for doctor for the detection of fungus in eye. The high surface area of activated carbon leads to high sensitivity. The device is later on calibrated up to a size of small sim and the standardization of the fungal strain, concentration and response time of the device is going on.

Technologies Used

Arduino, Signal processing, Sensors
