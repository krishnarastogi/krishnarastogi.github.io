Krishna Rastogi

Studies on heart related and orthopedic activities using MPAQ and DAAFBO equipment

Monitoring the chronically ill in their daily lives provides more reliable information that the patient’s self-report or doctor’s visit alone. This enhances the quality of the treatment and rehabilitation methods. The system can simultaneously measure several parameters in, on and around the human body from temperature, heart rate and brain activity to the intensity of muscle activity. The difference is in the extremely high quality of the data and it can simultaneously measure 32 different parameters

Technical Implementations

ECG Signal analysis, Signal Processing, Arduino
