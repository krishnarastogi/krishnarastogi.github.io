A Low Cost Non Invasive Electroretinography To Detect Abnormality Of Retina

A stimulus originating from retina of the eye and subsequent signal processing using MATLAB platform was carried out. The intent of the project was to analyze the response of rod and cone cells of the eye. Associated prototype was developed for the purpose of demonstration in the Texas Instruments Innovation Challenge INDIA ANALOG DESIGN CONTEST 2014

Technical Implementations

Texas intrument MSP430, Electrical simulation, Signal processing
